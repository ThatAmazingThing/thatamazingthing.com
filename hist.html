We celebrated 20 years of That Amazing Thing! with a stream from the Carlton Club (since that's all we can do at mo) back in July. In promotion of that (& general celebration of the birthday) a couple of us messed (for bloody AGES) creating the wordcloud above. The underlying data was a spreadsheet we've had knocking round half-done for years. Now we have it near finished & present it below. "near" finished as there may be a few inaccuracies - we'd like people to shout us any corrections they know of. Which years did Matt Rushton play the Camping Trip? At which Corbieres party did "Katkin" guest? There are some omissions & best guesses in finally getting this out, but we reckon it's a generally accurate record & makes us happy to look at.
